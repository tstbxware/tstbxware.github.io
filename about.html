About Toastbox Software

We're a group of teenagers (there's 4 of us in the team currently) who are all residing in Asia. We aim to explore the world of open source programming, reverse engineering, and software porting; but our approach doesn't just stop there.

We also channel our creativity by making small but entertaining indie titles (under the name Toastbox Interactive), and at the same time we also make useful open source software, utilities, and breaking the limits of your handheld devices. Anyone is welcome to contribute to our projects.
